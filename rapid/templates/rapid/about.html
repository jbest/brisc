About BRISC

BRISC is being developed at the Botanical Research Institute of Texas (BRIT) and is released under an open source license at https://github.com/jbest/brisc

About the BRIT Inventory

BRISC was used for the inventory of the BRIT herbarium in 2015. The inventory was completed with the help of 136 participants (volunteers and staff) who spent 1,927 person hours inventorying. For details and results of this inventory, please visit www.brit.org/herbarium/inventory.

Acknowledgements
